
File:NOAA fishery science center blood sample bonnethead shark pup.JPGFish medicine is the study and treatment of the diseases of fish. Although some practitioners work primarily with aquarium fish this field also has important applications to fisheries management.

Fish medicine is a relatively recent veterinary specialization; veterinary textbooks in the English language were not published until the early 1990s. The United States does not have an official Veterinary specialties
