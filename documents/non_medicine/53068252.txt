'''''Ssanghwa-tang''''' ({{Korean|hangul=쌍화탕|hanja=雙和湯|labels=no|links=no}}) or '''''ssanghwa-cha''''' ({{Korean|hangul=쌍화차|hanja=雙和茶|labels=no|links=no}}) is a [[traditional Korean tea]] with deep brown colour and a slightly bitter taste. Along with sipjeondaebotang, it is one of the most popular types of medical tea in Korea.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://english.chosun.com/site/data/html_dir/2015/02/21/2015022100140.html|title=The Health Benefits of Traditional Korean Tea|date=21 February 2015|newspaper=[[The Chosun Ilbo]]|access-date=2 February 2017}}</ref>

It is made by boiling down a number of [[Traditional Chinese medicine|medical herbs]] such as dried roots of [[Paeonia obovata|white woodland peony]] ({{Korean|hangul=백작약|hanja=白芍藥|rr=baekjagyak|labels=no|links=no}}), steamed and dried roots of [[Rehmannia glutinosa|rehmannia]] ({{Korean|hangul=숙지황|hanja=熟地黃|rr=sukjihwang|labels=no|links=no}}), dried roots of [[Astragalus propinquus|Mongolian milkvetch]] ({{Korean|hangul=황기|hanja=黃芪|rr=hwanggi|labels=no|links=no}}), dried roots of [[Angelica gigas|Korean angelica]] ({{Korean|hangul=당귀|hanja=當歸|rr=danggwi|labels=no|links=no}}), dried roots of [[lovage]] ({{Korean|hangul=천궁|hanja=川芎|rr=cheongung|labels=no|links=no}}), [[Cinnamomum cassia|Chinese cinnamon]] barks ({{Korean|hangul=계피|hanja=桂皮|rr=gyepi|labels=no|links=no}}), and [[Glycyrrhiza uralensis|Chinese liquorice]] ({{Korean|hangul=감초|hanja=甘草|rr=gamcho|labels=no|links=no}}). However, many people today buy the tea pre-made.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://english.visitkorea.or.kr/enu/FO/FO_EN_6_3_3_2.jsp|title=The World of Korean Tea – Medicinal Tea|website=[[Korea Tourism Organization]]|access-date=2 February 2017}}</ref>

== Efficacy and administration methods ==
Ssanghwangtang is a herbal bath which was enjoyed in the morning and evening during the Joseon Dynasty. Precautions for use should be consulted with a doctor, pharmacist, etc. before taking it, such as patients with hypertension, heart failure or kidney disorder, edema patients, and patients receiving medical treatment.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://m.mhj21.com/111469|title=Considering your health, Ssanghwa-tang|date=2018-01-11|work=문화·산업·경제강국을 향한 [문화저널21]|access-date=2018-04-17}}</ref>
