The '''Swedish Veterinary Disciplinary Board''' ({{lang-sv|Veterinära ansvarsnämnden}}) was a [[Government agencies in Sweden|Swedish government agency]] that answered to the Ministry of Agriculture, Food and Consumer Affairs. The agency tried cases of professional misconduct among [[veterinarian]]s. It was located in [[Jönköping Municipality|Jönköping]].
