The '''Chief Veterinary Officer''' of Hungary, as a [[Chief Veterinary Officer|chief veterinary officer]], is the supervisor of the [[National Food Chain Safety Office]], and in general, the governmental veterinary services including [[animal health]], [[animal rights|animal protection]] and welfare and food chain safety control. Besides organizing and operating [[veterinary public health]], he plays a significant role in international agricultural and trade affairs, in which the animal health and [[food safety]] status usually an important issue. His responsibilities are detailed in the law XLVI of 2008 on food chain and its authority control.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://net.jogtar.hu/jr/gen/hjegy_doc.cgi?docid=a0800046.tv|title=2008. évi XLVI. törvény - az élelmiszerláncról és hatósági felügyeletéről|last=Kft.|first=Wolters Kluwer|access-date=2017-10-17|language=en}}</ref>
