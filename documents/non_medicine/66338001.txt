'''''Gracey's Meat Hygiene''''' is a veterinary textbook which deals with [[meat inspection]] and meat hygiene control. The eleventh edition of the book was published in 2015.<ref>{{cite web |title=Gracey's Meat Hygiene, 11th Edition {{!}} Wiley |url=https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Gracey%27s+Meat+Hygiene%2C+11th+Edition-p-9781118650028 |website=Wiley.com |language=en-us}}</ref>
