'''''The Fantastic All-Electric Music Movie''''', also known as ''John Wilson's Mini Musicals'', is an anthology of short animated music videos which were originally created by John Wilson for ''[[The Sonny & Cher Comedy Hour|The Sonny and Cher Show]]'' during the early to mid-1970s.

The film was released on home video in 1981 by Video Gems under license from Fine Arts Films, an animation company founded by John Wilson in 1955. As well as the sixteen animated music videos from the show, the film includes an animated short based on [[Igor Stravinski]]'s ''[[Petrushka (ballet)|Petrushka]]''.<ref>[http://www.fineartsfilms.com John Wilson's Fine Arts Films Site]</ref>

==Content description==
Animator John Wilson produced animated music video shorts for original songs and covers of original songs sung by Sonny Bono and Cher. The songs "[[Big Yellow Taxi]]" and "[[Both Sides Now]]" are sung by [[Joni Mitchell]]. "Higher Ground" is covered by [[Sonny and Cher]].

The animated music video for [[Joni Mitchell]]'s ''Both Sides Now'' utilizes ground-breaking [[computer animation]].
