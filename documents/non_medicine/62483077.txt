Throughout [[History of China|Chinese history]], China had multiple periods of [[Golden age (metaphor)|golden age]]. In [[Chinese historiography]], golden ages on a large scale are known as {{transl|zh|shèngshì}} ({{lang|zh-Hant|盛世}}, lit. Prosperous Era), while golden ages on a smaller scale are termed as {{transl|zh|zhìshì}} ({{lang|zh-Hant|治世}}, lit. Well-Governed Era).

==List of Chinese golden ages==
This is a list of the golden ages in the history of China, sorted by [[Dynasties in Chinese history|dynasty]].
