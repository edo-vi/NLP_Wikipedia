'''''Zeitschrift für Evidenz, Fortbildung und Qualität im Gesundheitswesen''''' (English: '''''The Journal of Evidence and Quality in Health Care''''') is a bilingual German and English language [[Peer Review|peer-reviewed]] [[medical journal]] published by [[Elsevier|Elsevier Germany]].
It was established in 1904 as the ''Zeitschrift für ärztliche Fortbildung'' (''German Journal for Continuous Medical Education'') by [[Ernst von Bergmann]]. 
ZEFQ is the organ of various organisations working in the fields of [[evidence-based medicine]], [[patient empowerment]], [[patient safety]], and [[quality management]] in healthcare:.<ref>German Network for Evidence based Medicine [http://www.ebm-netzwerk.de/was-wir-tun/publikationen/zefq About the German Journal for Evidence and Quality in Health Care]</ref> Among these are:
* [[Association of the Scientific Medical Societies in Germany]] (AWMF), 
* [[Federal Joint Committee (Germany)|Federal Joint Committee]] (G-BA), 
* [[German Agency for Quality in Medicine]] (AEZQ),
* [[German Coalition for Patient Safety]] (APS)
* [[Cochrane Collaboration|German Cochrane Centre]], 
* [[German Network for Evidence Based Medicine]] (DNEbM), 
* [[IQWIG|Institute for Quality and Efficiency in Healthcare]] (IQWIG), 
* [[Guidelines International Network]] (G-I-N),
*  AQUA-Institute on Applied Quality Improvement and Research in Health Care,<ref>AQUA-Institute: [http://www.aqua-institut.de/en/home/index.html About AQUA]</ref>
* Drug Commission of the German Medical Association (AkdAE),<ref>AkdAE: [http://www.akdae.de/en/index.html About AkdAE]</ref>
* German Society for Quality Management in Healthcare (GQMG),<ref>GQMG: [http://www.gqmg.de/ Homepage]</ref>
* Medical Advisory Service of the German Health Insurance (MDS),<ref>MDS: [http://www.mds-ev.de/ Homepage]</ref>

ZEFQ's [[editor in chief]] was [[Günter Ollenschläger]] from 1995 until 12/2015, succeeded by [[Gabriele Meyer]]
