The '''German Agency for Quality in Medicine (AEZQ)''' - in German "Ärztliches Zentrum für Qualität in der Medizin (ÄZQ)", established in 1995 and located in [[Berlin]], co-ordinates [[healthcare]] quality programmes with special focus on [[evidence-based medicine]], [[medical guideline]]s, [[patient empowerment]], [[patient safety]] programs, and [[quality management]].<ref>World Health Organization - Europe. Health Evidence Network (HEN). Technical Members: [http://www.euro.who.int/en/what-we-do/data-and-evidence/health-evidence-network-hen/technical-members/current-technical-members/german-agency-for-quality-in-medicine-aqumed,-germany German Agency for Quality in Medicine] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110531101748/http://www.euro.who.int/en/what-we-do/data-and-evidence/health-evidence-network-hen/technical-members/current-technical-members/german-agency-for-quality-in-medicine-aqumed,-germany |date=31 May 2011 }}</ref>

AEZQ is a non-profit organization owned by the [[German Medical Association]] and the [[National Association of Statutory Health Insurance Physicians]].<ref>{{cite book |first1=Helena |last1=Legido-Quigley |author-link1=Helena Legido-Quigley|first2=Martin |last2=McKee |first3=Ellen |last3=Nolte |first4=Irene A |last4=Glinos |year=2008 |chapter=Germany |chapter-url=https://books.google.com/books?id=VUB3zSEm4DcC&pg=PA116 |title=Assuring the quality of health care in the European Union: A case for action |url=http://www.euro.who.int/__data/assets/pdf_file/0007/98233/E91397.pdf |publisher=World Health Organization |isbn=978-92-890-7193-2 |pages=116–20}}</ref>

==Activities==
AEZQ initiated several quality programs for the German healthcare system:

It established the German Program for [[evidence-based medicine|evidence based]] [[medical guideline]]s in the late 90s. Based upon guideline standards from the [[Scottish Intercollegiate Guideline Network]] and referring to experiences from the [[National Guideline Clearinghouse]] in the US, the agency founded a German Guideline [[wikt:clearing house|Clearinghouse]]<ref>{{cite journal |first1=G. |last1=Ollenschläger |first2=H. |last2=Kirchner |first3=U. |last3=Oesingmann |first4=F. W. |last4=Kolkmann |first5=R. |last5=Kunz |year=2001 |title=The German Guidelines Clearing house (GGC) – rationale, aims and results |journal=Proceedings of the Royal College of Physicians of Edinburgh |volume=31 |issue=Supplement 9 |pages=59–64 |url=http://www.rcpe.ac.uk/journal/supplements/supplement-9.pdf}}</ref> aiming at best practice in guideline production.<ref>{{cite book |author=Council of Europe |year=2002 |title=Developing a methodology for drawing up guidelines on best medical practice |url=http://www.leitlinien.de/mdb/edocs/pdf/literatur/coe-rec-2001-13.pdf |location=Strasbourg |publisher=Council of Europe Publishing |isbn=978-92-871-4788-2 |pages=46–47 |access-date=5 April 2011 |archive-date=2 December 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191202140857/http://www.leitlinien.de/mdb/edocs/pdf/literatur/coe-rec-2001-13.pdf |url-status=dead }}</ref>

In 1998 AEZQ was co-founder of the [[German Network for Evidence Based Medicine]].{{cn|date=December 2022}}

The organization established the German Clearinghouse for [[patient information leaflets|Patient Information]]<ref>{{cite journal |pages=391–7 |doi=10.1055/s-2002-32812 |title=Gut informiert über Gesundheitsfragen, aber wie? - Das Deutsche Clearingverfahren für Patienteninformationen - Zielsetzung, Hintergrund und Arbeitsweise |trans-title=Well-informed on health matters--how well? The German 'Clearinghouse for Patient Information'--objective, background and methods |language=de |year=2002 |last1=Sänger |first1=S |last2=Nickel |first2=J |last3=Huth |first3=A |last4=Ollenschläger |first4=G |journal=Das Gesundheitswesen |volume=64 |issue=7 |pmid=12119584}}</ref> in order to promote scientifically based [[shared decision making]]<ref>{{cite journal |pmid=11493320 |pmc=5060064 |year=2001 |last1=Delbanco |first1=T |last2=Berwick |first2=DM |last3=Boufford |first3=JI |last4=Edgman-Levitan |first4=S |last5=Ollenschläger |first5=G |last6=Plamping |first6=D |last7=Rockefeller |first7=RG |title=Healthcare in a land called PeoplePower: nothing about me without me |volume=4 |issue=3 |pages=144–50 |journal=Health Expectations |doi=10.1046/j.1369-6513.2001.00145.x}}</ref> in 1999.

In 2002, the agency set up the National Program for Disease Management Guidelines.<ref>{{cite journal |pages=383–7 |doi=10.1007/s00063-007-1052-1 |title=Nationale VersorgungsLeitlinien von BÄK, AWMF und KBV |trans-title=The German program for disease management guidelines. Results and perspectives |language=de |year=2007 |last1=Ollenschläger |first1=Günter |last2=Kopp |first2=Ina |journal=Medizinische Klinik |volume=102 |issue=5 |pmid=17497089|s2cid=31255706 }}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |pages=533–9 |doi=10.1016/j.zefq.2010.07.010 |title=7 Jahre Nationale VersorgungsLeitlinien (NVL) - Quo vadis? |trans-title=After seven years of National Disease Management Guidelines: quo vadis? |language=de |year=2010 |last1=Weinbrenner |first1=Susanne |last2=Conrad |first2=Susann |last3=Weikert |first3=Beate |last4=Kopp |first4=Ina |journal=Zeitschrift für Evidenz, Fortbildung und Qualität im Gesundheitswesen |volume=104 |issue=7 |pmid=21095605}}</ref>

The development of reliable and consumer information is integral part of this guideline program.<ref>{{cite journal |pages=578–84 |doi=10.1016/j.zefq.2010.09.001 |title=Gut informiert entscheiden: Patienten- und Verbraucherinformationen am ÄZQ |trans-title=Informed decisions: patient and consumer information at the German Agency for Quality in Medicine (ÄZQ) |language=de |year=2010 |last1=Schaefer |first1=Corinna |last2=Kirschning |first2=Silke |journal=Zeitschrift für Evidenz, Fortbildung und Qualität im Gesundheitswesen |volume=104 |issue=7 |pmid=21095611}}</ref>

AEZQ was co-founder of the [[Guidelines International Network]] in 2002 - under the leadership of [[Günter Ollenschläger]], collaborating with other national healthcare quality agencies like the US [[Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality]], the UK [[National Institute for Health and Clinical Excellence]], the Australian [[National Health and Medical Research Council]].<ref>{{cite journal |pages=455–60 |doi=10.1136/qhc.13.6.455 |title=Improving the quality of health care: using international collaboration to inform guideline programmes by founding the Guidelines International Network (G-I-N) |year=2004 |last1=Ollenschlager |first1=G |journal=Quality and Safety in Health Care |volume=13 |issue=6 |pmid=15576708 |last2=Marshall |first2=C |last3=Qureshi |first3=S |last4=Rosenbrand |first4=K |last5=Burgers |first5=J |last6=Mäkelä |first6=M |last7=Slutsky |first7=J |author8=Board of Trustees 2002, Guidelines International Network (G-I-N) |pmc=1743909}}</ref>

In the field of [[patient safety]] AEZQ was one of the first German organisations calling for effective patient safety programs.<ref>{{citation |last=Ollenschläger |first=G. |year=2001 |title=Medizinische Risiken, Fehler und Patientensicherheit. Zur Situation in Deutschland |language=de |journal=Schweizerische Ärztezeitung |volume=82 |issue=26 |pages=1404–10 |doi=10.4414/saez.2001.08273|doi-access=free }}</ref> The agency was co-founder of the [[German Coalition for Patient Safety]]. AEZQ established a national web of [[patient safety|Critical Incident Reporting Systems]].<ref>{{cite journal |pages=92–9 |doi=10.3238/arztebl.2010.0092 |title=Patient Safety and Error Management—What Causes Adverse Events and How Can They Be Prevented? |journal=Deutsches Ärzteblatt International |first1=Barbara |last1=Hoffmann |first2=Julia |last2=Rohe |pmid=20204120 |year=2010 |volume=107 |issue=6 |pmc=2832110}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal |pages=563–71 |doi=10.1016/j.zefq.2010.08.002 |title=15 Jahre ÄZQ – 10 Jahre Patientensicherheit am ÄZQ |trans-title=After 15 years of ÄZQ: 10 years of safety for patients |year=2010 |last1=Rohe |first1=Julia |last2=Heinrich |first2=Andrea Sanguino |last3=Fishman |first3=Liat |last4=Renner |first4=Daniela |last5=Thomeczek |first5=Christian |journal=Zeitschrift für Evidenz, Fortbildung und Qualität im Gesundheitswesen |volume=104 |issue=7 |pmid=21095609 |language=de}}</ref> The institution is partner of the international [[High 5 Project]].

In 2010 the agency set up a [[Digital Library]] for physicians and other interested health professionals and lays - the German Medical eLibrary (www.arztbibliothek.de) - for reinforcement of reliable and unbiased [[knowledge management]] in healthcare.<ref>{{cite journal |pages=585–90 |doi=10.1016/j.zefq.2010.07.005 |title=Die Arztbibliothek |trans-title=The German Medical eLibrary |language=de |year=2010 |last1=Rütters |first1=Dana |last2=Schwencke |first2=Silja |last3=Bunk |first3=Thomas |last4=Nothacker |first4=Monika |journal=Zeitschrift für Evidenz, Fortbildung und Qualität im Gesundheitswesen |volume=104 |issue=7 |pmid=21095612}}</ref>

AEZQ entertained the administrative offices of the [[Guidelines International Network]], and of the [[German Network for Evidence Based Medicine]] until 2013. It hosts the editorial office of the [[German Journal for Evidence and Quality in Healthcare ZEFQ]].{{cn|date=December 2022}}
