'''''Maytenus robusta''''',<ref>{{Cite web|title=Maytenus robusta Reissek — The Plant List|url=http://www.theplantlist.org/tpl1.1/record/tro-6600215|access-date=2020-06-22|website=www.theplantlist.org}}</ref> known in [[Brazil]] as '''''cafezinho do mato''''' or '''''coração de bugre''''', is a species of [[plant]] in the family [[Celastraceae]]. It is [[Endemism|endemic]] to Brazil. 

''Maytenus robusta'' is used in folk medicine to treat [[gastric ulcers]].<ref>A. Balbach A flora nacional na medicina doméstica Vida Plena, 2 (1980), p. 34</ref><ref>S.C. Cunha Ação farmacológica da espinheira-santa: usos e precauções Universidade Federal de Lavras, Lavras (2003), p. 100</ref><ref>{{cite journal|last1=Niero|first1=Rivaldo|last2=Faloni de Andrade|first2=Sergio|last3=Cechinel Filho|first3=Valdir|title=A Review of the Ethnopharmacology, Phytochemistry and Pharmacology of Plants of the Maytenus Genus|journal=Current Pharmaceutical Design|date=1 June 2011|volume=17|issue=18|pages=1851–1871|doi=10.2174/138161211796391029|pmid=21554223}}</ref>
