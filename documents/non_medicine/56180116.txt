The '''Veterinary Hospital Managers Association''' is a professional organisation for [[veterinarian]] [[practice manager]]s.

The association's main objectives are to:

· Develop professional competence by offering relevant educational resources.<br>
· Promote and define veterinary practice management as a profession; promote certification as a standard.<br>
· Provide individuals with a network for professional connection and support.<ref>{{cite web |title=Our Mission and Values - VHMA |url=https://www.vhma.org/about-us/mission-values |website=www.vhma.org |publisher=Veterinary Hospital Managers Association |language=en}}</ref>

The American organisation is based in Florida and claims 2500 members.<ref>{{cite news|title=VBMA Member Page|url=http://www.vhma.org/?page=VBMA|accessdate=31 December 2017|publisher=Veterinary Hospital Managers Association}}</ref>

In 2018 the Veterinary Hospital Managers Association (VHMA) launched an online program to help prepare participants to earn their Certified Veterinary Practice Manager (CVPM) certification.<ref>{{cite news |title=Veterinary Hospital Managers Association opens management prep course |url=https://www.veterinarypracticenews.com/veterinary-hospital-managers-association-opens-management-prep-course/ |publisher=Veterinary Practice News |date=4 May 2018 |language=en-CA}}</ref>

The British organisation of the same name is based in [[Kettering]].<ref>{{cite web|title=About us|url=http://www.vpma.co.uk/about-the-vpma.html|website=Veterinary Practice Management Association|accessdate=31 December 2017}}</ref>
