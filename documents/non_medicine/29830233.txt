'''Depuratives''' are [[herb]]s that are considered to have [[Ritual purification|purifying]] and [[Detoxification|detoxifying]] effects.<ref>{{Cite book |title=The Ultimate Herbal Compendium |first=Kerry |last=Bone |publisher=Phyotherapy Press |year=2007 |isbn=978-0-646-47602-5 }}</ref>

Herbs that are considered depurative include [[Vaccinium vitis-idaea|lingonberry]], [[Calycopteris floribunda|ukshi]], the [[four-leaf clover]], ''[[Paris polyphylla]]'', and some species of [[Sambucus|elderberry]].
