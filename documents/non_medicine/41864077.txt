Dr. '''John R. Clifford''' is an American [[veterinarian]]. Since 1985 he has been with [[APHIS]], [[Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service]] of the [[United States Department of Agriculture]],<ref name=aphis>[http://www.aphis.usda.gov/wps/portal/banner/aboutaphis?1dmy&urile=wcm%3apath%3a%2FAPHIS_Content_Library%2FSA_About_APHIS%2FSA_APHIS_Leadership "APHIS Leadership and Biographies"]</ref> where he held various positions including USDA Chief Veterinary Officer.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://usda.gov/wps/portal/usda/usdahome?contentid=2012/04/0132.xml |title=Statement by USDA Chief Veterinary Officer John Clifford Regarding a Detection of Bovine Spongiform Encephalopathy (BSE) in the United States &#124; USDA Newsroom |publisher=Usda.gov |date=2012-04-24 |accessdate=2014-02-06}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=APHIS Deputy Administrator for Veterinary Services Dr. John Clifford on World Veterinary Year 2011 |url=http://blogs.usda.gov/2011/06/23/aphis-deputy-administrator-for-veterinary-services-dr-john-clifford-on-world-veterinary-year-2011/|website=USDA Blog|publisher=USDA|accessdate=10 October 2014}}</ref> Clifford received DVM and BS degrees in animal science from the [[University of Missouri]] and before joining APHIS he was a private veterinarian.<ref name=aphis/>
