'''Veronika Sergeyevna Stepanova''' ({{lang-ru|Вероника Сергеевна Степанова}}; born 4 January 2001) is a Russian [[Cross-country skiing (sport)|cross-country skier]] who competes internationally with the Russian national team.

==Career==
Stepanova competed at three [[FIS Nordic Junior World Ski Championships|Junior World Championships]] from 2019 to 2021, winning individual gold in the 5 km freestyle technique in [[2021 Nordic Junior World Ski Championships|2021]], and two silver medals with the relay team in [[2019 Nordic Junior World Ski Championships|2019]] and 2021.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://tass.com/sport/1386329 |title=Russia announces roster of cross-country skiers for 2022 Winter Olympics in Beijing |website=tass.com |date=11 January 2022 |access-date=8 February 2022}}</ref> She represented [[Russian Olympic Committee athletes at the 2022 Winter Olympics]] in the [[Cross-country skiing at the 2022 Winter Olympics – Women's sprint|individual sprint]] and won a gold medal in the [[Cross-country skiing at the 2022 Winter Olympics – Women's 4 × 5 kilometre relay|women's 4 × 5 kilometre relay]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://olympics.com/beijing-2022/olympic-games/en/results/cross-country-skiing/athlete-profile-n1036664-veronika-stepanova.htm |title=Veronika Stepanova |website=olympics.com |access-date=8 February 2022}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Look: Best of day five action from the Beijing Winter Olympics|url=https://gulfnews.com/photos/sports/look-best-of-day-five-action-from-the-beijing-winter-olympics-1.1644402297383|access-date=2022-02-10|website=gulfnews.com|language=en}}</ref>

==Personal life==
During the meeting with Russian president [[Vladimir Putin]] on 26 April 2022, commemorating Russian medal winners at the [[2022 Winter Olympics]] and in the wake of the [[2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine|Russian invasion of Ukraine]], Stepanova said: "In my eyes, Russia is back strong, proud and successful again. Not everyone in the world likes her; it’s obvious. But we are on the right track, and we will definitely win, just as we won the Olympics."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.brytfmonline.com/hard-to-believe-vg/|title=- Hard to believe - VG|date=27 April 2022}}</ref>
