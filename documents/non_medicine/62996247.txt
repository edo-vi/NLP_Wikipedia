'''''The Lowe Files''''' is a series on the [[A&E (TV channel)|A&E Network]] that looks at unsolved mysteries within the United States. The show is hosted by [[Rob Lowe]] and his sons [[John Owen Lowe]] and Edward Matthew Lowe.

==Episodes==
The series currently has aired 9 episodes.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.thefutoncritic.com/showatch/lowe-files/listings/|title=Shows A-Z - The Lowe Files on A&E|website=TheFutonCritic.com}}</ref> The series was cancelled after one season.
