{{Nihongo|'''''Sekkotsu'''''|接骨}} or '''Judo therapy''' (柔道整復術) is the traditional Japanese art of [[Bonesetter|bone-setting]]. It has been used in many [[Japanese martial arts]] and has developed alongside [[Judo]] (柔道) into a licensed medical practice somewhat resembling [[chiropractic]] in Japan today.
