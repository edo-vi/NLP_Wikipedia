In [[geology]] a '''collage''' is a [[tectonostratigraphy|tectonostratigraphic unit]] characterized by its heterogeneity in terms of its [[lithology]], rock ages, or both. The term is usually applied to [[Precambrian]] tectonostratigraphic units. Collages form by [[plate tectonics|plate tectonic processes]] like [[accretion (geology)|accretion]] and [[continental collision]].
