'''Roman Nikolayevich Shirokov''' ({{lang-ru|Роман Николаевич Широ́ков}} {{IPA-ru|rɐˈman nʲɪkɐˈɫaɪvʲɪtɕ ʂɨˈrokəf|}}; born 6 July 1981) is a Russian international [[association football|football]] official and a former player. He is the manager and the general director of [[FC Leon Saturn Ramenskoye|Leon Saturn Ramenskoye]].

As a player, he played as a [[midfielder]].

==Club career==

===Early career===
Before signing for [[FC Zenit Saint Petersburg|Zenit Saint Petersburg]], Shirokov played for [[FC Khimki]], [[FC Rubin Kazan|Rubin Kazan]], [[FC Saturn Moscow Oblast (1946-2011)|Saturn Moscow Oblast]], [[FC Vidnoye]], [[FC Istra]], [[FC Moscow|Torpedo-ZIL Moscow]] and [[PFC CSKA Moscow|CSKA Moscow]]'s farm club.{{citation needed|date=March 2022}}

===Zenit Saint Petersburg===
Shirokov started his first season for Zenit Saint Petersburg as a [[centre-back]], as then-head coach [[Dick Advocaat]] was forced to fill the gaps left in the centre of defence after the departure of [[Martin Škrtel]] to Liverpool and severe injury to [[Nicolas Lombaerts]].{{citation needed|date=March 2022}}

===Spartak Moscow===
On 18 July 2014, it was announced that Shirokov had signed a contract with [[FC Spartak Moscow|Spartak Moscow]] as a [[free agent]].<ref name="fcsm">{{cite web|publisher=Spartak.com|script-title=ru:ШИРОКОВ: «ОТЕЦ ВСЮ ЖИЗНЬ БОЛЕЛ ЗА "СПАРТАК"»|url=http://spartak.com/main/news/romanshirokov/|language=ru|access-date=18 July 2014}}</ref> After a long recovery from an injury he made his debut for Spartak on 26 October 2014 in a game against [[FC Lokomotiv Moscow|Lokomotiv Moscow]], scoring a late equalizer in a 1–1 game. On 13 January 2015, he was loaned back to [[FC Krasnodar]].<ref name="Krasnoloan2015">{{cite web|publisher=[[FC Krasnodar]]|url=http://fckrasnodar.ru/team/news/object/?object_id=96054|script-title=ru:Роман Широков снова будет защищать цвета «Краснодара»|date=13 January 2015|language=ru|title = Roman Shirokov to Defend the Colors of FC Krasnodar Again. Official site FC Krasnodar}}</ref>

Shirokov was benched during the 2015–16 season, in order to not pay him the amount due to him by contract if he plays a certain number of minutes.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.sports.ru/tribuna/blogs/dud/866726.html|title = "Спартак" против Широкова. Кто во всем виноват?}}</ref> The President of the [[Russian Football Union|RFU]], [[Vitaly Mutko]], commented: "He's one of the best footballers in our country. I don't understand why he's not playing. If it's due to the contract clause that's just dishonest."<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sports.ru/football/1035151089.html|title=Виталий Мутко: "Поговорю с руководством "Спартака" о Широкове. Ситуация тревожит"|website=Sports.ru}}</ref> On 23 January 2016, his Spartak contract was dissolved by mutual consent.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[FC Spartak Moscow]]|url=http://spartak.com/main/news/83367/|script-title=ru:«СПАРТАК» И ШИРОКОВ РАССТАЛИСЬ ПО ОБОЮДНОМУ СОГЛАСИЮ|date=23 January 2016|language=ru}}</ref>

===CSKA Moscow===
On 9 February 2016, he returned to his first club, [[PFC CSKA Moscow|CSKA Moscow]], signing a contract until the end of the 2015–16 season with an extension option.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[PFC CSKA Moscow]]|url=http://pfc-cska.com/novosti/vse-novosti/novosti-osnovy/roman-shirokov-v-pfk-cska/|script-title=ru:Роман Широков — в ПФК ЦСКА|date=9 February 2016|language=ru}}</ref> His contract was not extended at the end of the season.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[PFC CSKA Moscow]]|url=http://pfc-cska.com/novosti/vse-novosti/novosti-osnovy/pfk-cska-zhelaet-uspehov-shirokovu-i-olanare/|script-title=ru:ПФК ЦСКА желает успехов Широкову и Оланаре|date=13 July 2016|language=ru}}</ref>

==International career==
[[Image:RomanShirokov NT.jpg|thumb|left|170px|Shirokov preparing for a friendly against Bulgaria in August 2010]]

He was called up for the [[Euro 2008]] and started in the opening group match against [[Spain national football team|Spain]], being deployed in the centre of defence as then-manager of Russia [[Guus Hiddink]] decided to follow the footsteps of his compatriot [[Dick Advocaat|Advocaat]], thus relegating experienced [[Sergey Ignashevich]] to the bench. This move proved unsuccessful as Russia were beaten 1–4 with Shirokov at fault on the number of occasions, including Spaniards' first<ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.goal.com/en/news/476/euro-2008/2008/06/10/731076/player-ratings-spain-4-1-russia| title = Player Ratings: Spain 4-1 Russia {{!}} Goal.com}}</ref> and third<ref>{{cite news| url=http://www.skysports.com/football/euro2008/match-report/0,23136,13956_2925786,00.html | work=Sky Sports | title=Villa vanquishes Russians}}</ref> goals. After the game Hiddink slammed his team's performance in defence<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://euro2008.football.co.uk/russia/hiddink_slams_naive_russia_266097.shtml |title=Hiddink slams naive Russia - Russia Team News from football.co.uk |access-date=29 July 2009 |archive-date=9 August 2009 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090809134541/http://euro2008.football.co.uk/russia/hiddink_slams_naive_russia_266097.shtml |url-status=dead }}</ref> and revamped the squad, leaving Shirokov out of the first team for the rest of the tournament.

After a string of assuring performances in the centre of Zenit's midfield, he was called back to the national team in 2010 for a friendly against [[Bulgaria national football team|Bulgaria]]. 
He was confirmed for the finalized [[UEFA Euro 2012]] squad on 25 May 2012.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://rfs.ru/main/news/ct131/73916.html| language = ru | title = Advocaat announced the finalized Euro Squad|date=25 May 2012}}</ref> He scored the second goal of Russia's opening game of this tournament against the [[Czech Republic national football team|Czech Republic]] in a 4–1 win.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/18137693|title=Russia 4 Czech Republic 1|date=8 May 2012|work=BBC Sport|access-date=11 June 2012 }}</ref>

On 2 June 2014, he was included in Russia's [[2014 FIFA World Cup]] squad.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[Russian Football Union]]|url=http://rfs.ru/main/news/79322.html|script-title=ru:Состав национальной сборной России на ЧМ-2014|date=2 June 2014|language=ru}}</ref> He was excluded from the squad on 6 June due to injury.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[Russian Football Union]]|url=http://rfs.ru/main/news/national_teams/79375.html|script-title=ru:В Бразилию без Широкова|date=2 June 2014|language=ru}}</ref>

==Style of play==
Shirokov was described as "a deep-lying midfielder with an aptitude for getting forward to score goals".<ref>{{cite news|last=Wilson|first=Jonathan|title=Euro 2012 Preview: Russia|url=http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/2012/writers/jonathan_wilson/05/29/euro.2012.russia.preview/index.html|publisher=Sports Illustrated|access-date=2 June 2012|date=31 May 2012}}</ref>

==Post-playing career==
On 5 June 2018, Shirokov was appointed by [[FC Dynamo Moscow|Dynamo Moscow]] as the deputy general director in charge of sports.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[FC Dynamo Moscow]]|url=http://fcdynamo.ru/news/events/?id_4=13528|script-title=ru:Роман Широков – замгендиректора по спортивным вопросам|date=5 June 2018|language=ru}}</ref> He left Dynamo on 1 May 2019.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[FC Dynamo Moscow]]|url=http://fcdynamo.ru/news/events/?id_4=14196|script-title=ru:Роман Широков покинул «Динамо»|date=1 May 2019|language=ru}}</ref>

During an amateur game in August 2020 he attacked a referee with punches to his face and kicks once he had knocked the referee to the ground, causing the referee to be hospitalized for three weeks.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.themoscowtimes.com/2020/09/15/ex-russia-football-captain-shirokov-charged-with-punching-ref-a71450|title=Ex-Russia Football Captain Shirokov Charged With Punching Ref|first=The Moscow|last=Times|date=15 September 2020|website=The Moscow Times}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sportbible.com/football/football-news-roman-shirokov-could-be-tried-for-attempted-murder-after-ref-attack-20200915|title=Ex-Russia Captain Roman Shirokov Could Be Tried For Attempted Murder After Sickening Referee Attack|website=www.sportbible.com}}</ref><ref name="auto1">{{Cite web|url=https://www.eurosport.com/football/former-russia-and-zenit-star-roman-shirokov-sentenced-to-community-service-after-assault-on-referee_sto8045522/story.shtml|title=Former Russia and Zenit star Roman Shirokov sentenced to community service after assault on referee|date=26 December 2020|website=Eurosport}}</ref> He was reported to the police for assault.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mirror.co.uk/sport/football/news/roman-shirokov-referee-attack-russia-22501948|title=Russia's Euro 2016 captain hospitalises referee after brutal attack|website=[[Daily Mirror]]|date=11 August 2020}}</ref> He was tried and found guilty by a Moscow court.<ref name="auto1"/> He was sentenced to 100 hours of community service.<ref name="auto1"/> He was also suspended by his employer, Match TV.<ref name="auto1"/>

On 2 March 2022, Shirokov supported the [[2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine|Russian invasion of Ukraine]], adding that [[Anti-war protests in Russia (2022–present)|those Russians]], who are ashamed of the war, were welcome to leave the country.<ref>{{cite news |title=Роман Широков: "Поддерживаю действия Путина и армию России. Кому стыдно быть русским – валите на Запад" |url=https://www.sports.ru/football/1107347733-roman-shirokov-podderzhivayu-dejstviya-putina-armii-rossii-moya-podder.html |work=sports.ru |language=ru}}</ref>

On 6 March 2023, Shirokov was appointed general director of [[FC Saturn Ramenskoye|Saturn Ramenskoye]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Championat|url=https://www.championat.com/football/news-5021913-roman-shirokov-stal-generalnym-direktorom-saturna.html|title=Роман Широков назначен генеральным директором «Сатурна»|date=6 March 2023|language=ru}}</ref> On 31 August 2023, he took over as the club head coach as well.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Leon Saturn|url=https://www.saturn-fc.ru/news/30150/|title=Широков занял пост главного тренера|date=31 August 2023|language=ru}}</ref>
