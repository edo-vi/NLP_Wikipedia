The '''Indonesian Veterinary Medical Association''' ([[Indonesian language|Indonesian]]: '''{{lang|id|Perhimpunan Dokter Hewan Indonesia}}''') is a [[non-profit]], [[professional organization]] representing [[veterinary medicine]] in [[Indonesia]].<ref name="PANJANG " /> The association was established on 9 January 1953 in [[Lembang]], [[West Java]].<ref name="PANJANG">UPTD. PUSKESWAN PADANG PANJANG.2014.Profil Perhimpunan Dokter Hewan Indonesia (PDHI).</ref> Its activities include professional [[continuing education]], [[accreditation]], and maintaining a professional [[code of conduct]] for its members.<ref name=PDHI_profil>{{cite web | url=http://pdhi-online.org/?profil |title=Profil PDHI |publisher=Perhimpunan Dokter Hewan Indonesia online|accessdate=4 September 2014}}</ref> The association's headquarters are located in [[Jakarta]].
