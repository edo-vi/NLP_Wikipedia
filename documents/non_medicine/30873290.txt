'''Stomach''', a concept from [[traditional Chinese medicine]] as distinct from the Western medical concept of [[stomach]], is more a way of describing a set of interrelated parts than an anatomical organ.

The Stomach and its paired organ, the [[Spleen (Chinese medicine)|Spleen]], are associated with the element of earth and the emotions of anxiety and stress.<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Wu|first=Xie-Ning|date=1998-02-15|title=Current concept of Spleen-Stomach theory and Spleen deficiency syndrome in TCM|journal=World Journal of Gastroenterology|volume=4|issue=1|pages=2â€“6|doi=10.3748/wjg.v4.i1.2|issn=1007-9327|pmc=4767755|pmid=11819216 |doi-access=free }}</ref>
