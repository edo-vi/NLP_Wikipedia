'''Triphala''' ("three fruits") is an [[Ayurvedic]] [[herb]]al [[rasayana]] formula consisting of equal parts of three [[myrobalans]], taken without seed: [[Amalaki]] (''[[Phyllanthus emblica]]''), [[Beleric|Bibhitaki]] (''[[Terminalia bellirica]]''), and [[Haritaki]] (''[[Terminalia chebula]]'').
