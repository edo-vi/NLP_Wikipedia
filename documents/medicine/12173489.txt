The '''International Herb Symposium''' is a biannual conference held at [[Wheaton College (Massachusetts)|Wheaton College]] in [[Norton, MA|Norton]], [[Massachusetts]] to discuss botanical medicine, herbal conservation and the survival of [[First World]] herbal traditions.<ref>{{cite web|title=Welcome to the 11th International Herb Symposium Schedule of Events|url=http://www.internationalherbsymposium.com/uploads/files/11th_IHS_Schedule.pdf|publisher=International Herb Symposium|accessdate=20 November 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131005020025/http://www.internationalherbsymposium.com/uploads/files/11th_IHS_Schedule.pdf|archive-date=5 October 2013|url-status=dead}}</ref> This conference is one of the largest herb conferences held in the United States and the only worldwide herbal conference with an explicit international focus, including speakers from [[Africa]], [[Tibet]], Central and [[South America]] and [[Indigenous peoples of the Americas|American Indian]] tribes. During this conference, over 100 workshops are typically presented during a single weekend â€“ ranging from indigenous traditional to scientific uses of botanicals.<ref>{{cite web|title=International Herb Symposium|url=http://cms.herbalgram.org/searchresult.html?searchfor=%22international%20herb%20symposium%22&option=all&KY_WS_LOW=2636%7C2631%7C2626%7C2627%7C2629%7C2633%7C2638%7C2641%7C2888%7C6543|work=American Botanical Council|accessdate=20 November 2013}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=8th International Herb Symposium ~ 2007|url=http://www.treefarmtapes.com/catalog/conference.asp?conferenceid=745|work=Tree Farm Tapes|accessdate=20 November 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150610220404/http://www.treefarmtapes.com/catalog/conference.asp?conferenceid=745|archive-date=10 June 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref> The conference is organized by [[Rosemary Gladstar]] and Sage Mountain Retreat Center and features speakers from all over the world.<ref>{{cite web|title=Sage Mountain Retreat Center and Botanical Sanctuary|url=http://www.sagemountain.com/|accessdate=20 November 2013}}</ref>
