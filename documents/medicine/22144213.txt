'''''Evidence-Based Mental Health''''' is a quarterly [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[medical journal]] covering all aspects of [[mental health]]. It is co-owned by the BMJ Group, the [[Royal College of Psychiatrists]], and the [[British Psychological Society]].
