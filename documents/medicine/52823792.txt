'''''Cheongsimhwan''''' ({{lang-ko|청심환}}, {{literal|clear-mind pill}}), also called '''''uhwang-cheongsimhwan''''' ({{lang|ko|우황청심환}}) and '''''cheongsimwon''''' ({{lang|ko|청심원}}), is a pill formulated with thirty odd herbs and other medicinal ingredients, including ''[[calculus bovis]]'', [[ginseng]], [[musk]], and [[Chinese yam]] root.
