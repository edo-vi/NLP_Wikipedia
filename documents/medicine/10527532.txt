'''Harvey Wickes Felter''' (1865–1927) was an [[eclectic medicine]] doctor and author of ''[[Eclectic Materia Medica]]''.  He was co-author, with [[John Uri Lloyd]], of ''[[King's American Dispensatory]]''.
