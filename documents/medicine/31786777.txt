{{Short description|Steam herbal therapy in Ayurvedic treatment}}
{{Alternative medicine sidebar}}
'''Svedana''' or Swedana, means to "perspire". It is used in Ayurvedic treatment. It is also called as steam therapy.  There are different types of swedana. Sitting in sun is also called as swedana. Swedana is a pre procedure for [[Panchakarma]]. [[Snehana]] and [[Swedana]] are pre procedures for Panchakarma.
