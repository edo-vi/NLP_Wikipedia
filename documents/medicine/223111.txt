'''Paul Pétard''' (1912–1980) was a French [[botany|botanist]] who specialized in the study of native plants of [[French Polynesia]]. His book ''Petard Botanical Plant Encyclopedia'' is still widely used as a reference, and contains much information about traditional applications of [[Tahiti]]an [[Noni]] juice. He held a doctorate in [[pharmacy]].
