'''Basti''' is an [[enema]] of [[herbalism|herbal]] [[Essential_oil#Uses and cautions|oils]] or [[decoction]]s used in [[Ayurveda]].

Basti is one of the five Pradhana Karmas of [[Panchakarma]] and it is used to treat [[dosha|vata]] disorders.
