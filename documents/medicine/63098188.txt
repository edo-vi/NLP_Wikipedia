{{Short description|Viral disease of cats}}
'''Limping calici''' is a viral disease of cats that shows itself in kittens. It shows as a period of limping and inability to move about, that can cause death, but with the appropriate medical care tends to last about a week.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.kittenlady.org/limpingcalici|title=Limping Calici|website=Kitten Lady}}</ref>
