'''Bôtchô''' is a cream sold in [[Côte d'Ivoire]] allegedly to increase breast and buttock size, which has become increasingly popular due to the [[Bobaraba]] (big bottom) dance.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.babelgum.com/9256/afp-ivory-coasts-dangerous-cosmetics-craze-20-august.htm|title=Ivory Coast's dangerous cosmetics craze 20 August|work=[[Babelgum]]|date=2007-08-21|accessdate=2008-11-01|agency=AFP}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/africa/7233565.stm|title=Ivory Coast's 'big-bottom' craze |last=James|first=John|date=2008-02-18|publisher=BBC.com|accessdate=2008-11-01}}</ref>

''Bôtchô'' is also the title of an album by the Ivorian band [[Tchagbazu Boys]].
