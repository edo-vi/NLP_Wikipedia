The Journal of Evidence-Based Medicine is a quarterly peer-reviewed medical journal covering evidence-based medicine. It was established in 2008 and is published by John Wiley & Sons on behalf of the Chinese Cochrane Centre of which it is the official English-language journal. It grew out of a decade of work by editors of the Chinese Journal of Evidence Based Medicine to create a new journal with a global audience.
