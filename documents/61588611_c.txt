Metamorphic series include the Barrovian and Buchan series of metamorphic rocks. George Barrow (geologist)
File:Al2SiO5_phase_diagram.svgFile:Metamorphic Facies Series-Barrowian Buchan.png
* Buchan metamorphism has the facies series greenschist-amphibolite-granulite
* Barrovian metamorphism has the same facies series but has approximately 1&nbsp;kbar more pressure so these rocks form kyanite
